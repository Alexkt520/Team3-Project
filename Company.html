About us

Eloheh Healthcare takes “Caring for Life, Caring for Health” as its core philosophy, and undertakes a wide range of services such as disaster relief in the United States, production of biotechnology products, provision of Chinese and Western medicinal herbs, and production of medical devices.

The company was established with the year 1877 and is located in West Lafayette, Indiana, USA. The company has grown to include six brands, namely Medical One, Medical Two, Medical Three, Medical Four, Medical Five, and Medical Six, among other companies. There are more than 1,700 subsidiaries spread across the country.

The company not only owns production enterprises, scientific research and design research institutes, herb planting bases and a marketing network set up in various large, medium and small cities basically covering the whole country.During the epidemic of 2020, the company assumed the responsibility of rescue and disaster relief. The company has accumulated a total of 300 million nucleic acid test production, for the country completely do their part.
